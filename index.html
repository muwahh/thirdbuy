The process

From trade-in to your doorstep, five checkpoints deep.

This is the actual sequence every device moves through — nothing ships until it clears the last one.

01
Intake
Devices are logged by serial number and checked against theft and recall databases before anything else happens.
02
47-point diagnostic
Battery health, screen calibration, port continuity, camera and mic function, and structural wear are tested and logged.
03
Grading
Each device is assigned a grade — A, B, or C — based strictly on diagnostic results, not appearance alone.
04
Data wipe & certification
A NIST-standard data wipe is verified, and a certification report is generated and attached to the listing.
05
Ship with warranty
Every device leaves with its inspection report and a 12-month warranty, active from the day it arrives.
